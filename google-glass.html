Screen

How the world will appear

According to sources close to Google, the glasses will have a small LCD or AMOLED screen at the top and in front of the user's eyes. Using a camera and GPS, you can orient yourself, as well as select options with head movements.

Camera

Film and photograph at any time

With Google Glass it will be possible to take photos with up to 5 megapixels and record videos with 720 lines of resolution. The first videos and photos captured with the device have already been released online, but so far no one has much technical information.

Sensors

The sensitivity of a simple pair of glasses

Anyone who thinks that to control Google Glass they will need a keyboard and mouse is completely wrong. The device comes with several types of built-in sensors and microphones. Thus, to give a command, simply speak or swipe your finger on the side of the glasses.

Battery and Gadgets

What devices complement Glass?

According to Google itself, Glass will come with a battery that has enough autonomy to last a whole day. Only some activities such as video conferencing and long filming will require a little more. In addition, it comes with WiFi, Bluetooth, 3G/4G and much more.
